Our Story

The company milling facility is located in Miti Mingi- Pipeline, Nakuru County. Chamwa Quality Millers Ltd. sources its raw materials from small-scale farmers and traders within the county. Small-scale farmers supply maize during the harvest season while traders supply maize during the off-season. The company is however, registered with Agriculture Food Authority (AFA) and can import maize in case of any shortage in the local market.

The milling company has installed a semi-automatic milling machine with a capacity of producing 20 tonnes of flour per day. The machine is a complete line that can finish all maize flour production processes efficiently from the elevation of raw materials, selection by sifting, removal of micro impurities, stone, metals, moisture adjustment, peeling, corn germ removal, crushing, slagging, flour milling, and packaging. The machine also has a micro-doser, which fortifies the final maize flour. To ensure food safety during conditioning or dry milling the company sources its water from Discovery Spring Water.

With a well storage capacity of 3000 of 90 kg bags of maize, Chamwa Quality Millers operate with an emphasis on the availability of maize and delivering high-quality, fast-moving consumer goods. The company is committed to enhancing the community through long-term economic growth and development.

What Makes Us Unique

Chamwa Quality Millers prioritizes high-quality production and maximum customer satisfaction. Our maize flour meets regulatory, consumer, and producer standards. The company is registered under the Companies Act, holds a KRA PIN, is registered with the Agriculture Food Authority (AFA), and certified by the Kenya Bureau of Standards (KEBS).

We ensure maize moisture content remains between 13.5%–14% to prevent aflatoxin contamination. Regular laboratory testing is conducted to ensure proper fortification with essential micronutrients such as Iron and Zinc. Monthly testing is also conducted at JKUAT laboratories to ensure food safety and compliance.

We focus on affordability and accessibility, targeting small-scale retailers and households, including urban areas, with flexible packaging options.
