Who We Are

Established in 2022, Chamwa Quality Millers specializes in milling high-quality maize, producing fortified flour, and supplying by-products to animal feed manufacturers.

Our Mission

To produce fortified maize flour at affordable prices while supporting local farmers.

Our Vision

To deliver high-quality maize flour and drive economic growth for small-scale farmers.
